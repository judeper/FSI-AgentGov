# Control 4.3: Site and Document Retention Management

## Overview

**Control ID:** 4.3  
**Control Name:** Site and Document Retention Management  
**Regulatory Reference:** FINRA 4511, SEC 17a-3/4, GLBA 501(b), SOX 404  
**Setup Time:** 1-2 hours  

---

## Governance Levels

### Baseline (Level 1)

Apply retention labels to SharePoint sites; enforce retention policies.

### Recommended (Level 2-3)

Retention labels by content type; automated disposition reviews; deletion audit logs.

### Regulated/High-Risk (Level 4)

Policy-driven retention; no manual deletion; immutable deletion logs.

---

## Verification & Testing

1. SharePoint site → Settings → Site information → Retention label
2. Verify retention labels are applied
3. Check retention policy for sensitivity and content type
4. Confirm deletion audit logs
EXPECTED: Retention labels applied and enforced per regulation
