# Control 4.2: Site Access Reviews and Certification

## Overview

**Control ID:** 4.2  
**Control Name:** Site Access Reviews and Certification  
**Regulatory Reference:** GLBA 501(b), 504(b), 505(b), SOX 404, FINRA 4511  
**Setup Time:** 1-2 hours  

---

## Governance Levels

### Baseline (Level 1)

Conduct quarterly access reviews of SharePoint sites accessed by agents.

### Recommended (Level 2-3)

Automated access review workflow; owner certification of access; removal of unnecessary access.

### Regulated/High-Risk (Level 4)

Mandatory annual certification; auditor-signed attestation.

---

## Verification & Testing

1. Review site access review schedule and results
2. Check certification records
3. Verify removed access is documented
4. Confirm schedule is compliant (quarterly minimum)
EXPECTED: Access reviews conducted and documented regularly
