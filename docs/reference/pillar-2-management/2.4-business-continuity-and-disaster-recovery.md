# Control 2.4: Business Continuity and Disaster Recovery

## Overview

**Control ID:** 2.4  
**Control Name:** Business Continuity and Disaster Recovery  
**Regulatory Reference:** GLBA 501(b), SOX 404, FINRA 4511, OCC 2011-12  
**Setup Time:** 2-3 hours  

---

## Governance Levels

### Baseline (Level 1)

Document BC/DR plan for critical agents; backup configurations and dependencies.

### Recommended (Level 2-3)

Automated backup to secondary region; recovery time objective (RTO) <4 hours.

### Regulated/High-Risk (Level 4)

Geo-redundant backup with <1 hour RTO; annual disaster recovery test with documented results.

---

## Verification & Testing

1. Verify backup configuration for critical agents
2. Check backup schedule and retention
3. Review BC/DR plan documentation
4. Test recovery procedure (if possible)
EXPECTED: BC/DR plan documented; backups configured; recovery procedures tested
