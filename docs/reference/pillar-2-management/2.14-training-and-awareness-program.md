# Control 2.14: Training and Awareness Program

## Overview

**Control ID:** 2.14  
**Control Name:** Training and Awareness Program  
**Regulatory Reference:** FINRA 4512, SOX 404, GLBA 501(a), FINRA Notice 25-07  
**Setup Time:** 2-3 hours  

---

## Governance Levels

### Baseline (Level 1)

Provide training on agent governance policies to all creators and admins; annual refresher.

### Recommended (Level 2-3)

Role-specific training; documented completion records; compliance certification program.

### Regulated/High-Risk (Level 4)

Mandatory annual training with assessment; compliance certification required before agent deployment.

---

## Verification & Testing

1. Review training program documentation
2. Check training completion records
3. Verify assessments are passed
4. Confirm training is updated annually
EXPECTED: Training program in place; compliance tracked
