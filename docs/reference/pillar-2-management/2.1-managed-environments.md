# Control 2.1: Managed Environments

## Overview

**Control ID:** 2.1  
**Control Name:** Managed Environments  
**Regulatory Reference:** FINRA 4511, SEC 17a-4, GLBA 501(b), SOX 302  
**Setup Time:** 30-45 min  

---

## Governance Levels

### Baseline (Level 1)

Enable Managed Environments for Zone 2+ agents; configure governance policies.

### Recommended (Level 2-3)

Governance policies enforced at environment level; weekly governance audit.

### Regulated/High-Risk (Level 4)

Mandatory Managed Environments for Zone 3; real-time policy enforcement with immediate violation alerts.

---

## Verification & Testing

1. Power Platform Admin Center → Environments → [environment]
2. Verify Managed Environment is Enabled
3. Confirm governance policies are active
4. Check Governance Insights for compliance
EXPECTED: Managed Environment configured; policies enforced
