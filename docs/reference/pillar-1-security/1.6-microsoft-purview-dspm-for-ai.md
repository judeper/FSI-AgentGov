# Control 1.6: Microsoft Purview: DSPM for AI

## Overview

**Control ID:** 1.6  
**Control Name:** Microsoft Purview: DSPM for AI  
**Regulatory Reference:** FINRA Notice 25-07, SEC AI priorities, GLBA 501(a), SOX 302  
**Setup Time:** 1-2 hours  

---

## Governance Levels

### Baseline (Level 1)

Establish AI data handling policy and periodic manual reviews of sensitive data exposure risks.

### Recommended (Level 2-3)

Enable Microsoft Purview DSPM for AI for monitoring + policy insights; integrate with DLP/labels.

### Regulated/High-Risk (Level 4)

Real-time monitoring + automated escalation; integrate with SOC; evidence retention.
